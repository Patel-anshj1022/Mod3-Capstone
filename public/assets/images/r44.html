Robinson R44

The Robinson R44 is a single engine helicopter capable of carrying 3 passengers plus pilot utilising a 2+2 cabin configuration. Every passenger has a window seat ensuring great visibility. Primarily used for scenic flights, the R44’s economical running costs also make it a great choice for numerous airwork tasks like aerial film and photography including downlink (for live broadcast), and general charter requirements.
